# Maintainers

Maintainers review and merge changes, triage issues, and own the long-term health of AgentOS. A review from any one maintainer can approve a change. Reach the team at team@frame.dev or open an [issue](https://github.com/framerslab/agentos/issues).

| Maintainer | GitHub | Focus |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Johnny Dunn | [@jddunn](https://github.com/jddunn) | Runtime, cognitive memory, orchestration, releases |
| Victor Evogor | [@Victor-Evogor](https://github.com/Victor-Evogor) | Orchestration, core runtime |

Review routing is defined in [.github/CODEOWNERS](.github/CODEOWNERS).

## Becoming a maintainer

Maintainers are added after sustained, high-quality contributions and a track record of sound review judgment. Start by contributing (see [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md)) and stay engaged with reviews and issues.
